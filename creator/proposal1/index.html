La plataforma digital de minerales

Un entorno unificado para onboarding de proveedores, compra de minerales, análisis de laboratorio, inventario, logística y operaciones financieras en la cadena de suministro polimetálica.

Gestión de proveedores

Centralize onboarding and risk scoring for mining companies and small miners, including KYC, AML and ESG documentation.

  • Digital miner profiles
  • Compliance document vault
  • Risk and performance scoring

Compra de minerales

Register polymetallic lots with assay data, negotiate digitally, and close contracts in a compliant, auditable workflow.

  • Lot registration and validation
  • Automated pricing suggestions
  • Digital RFQs and bids

Análisis de laboratorio

Integrate certified laboratories for chemical analysis and automatic import of assays and certificates.

  • Lab work order generation
  • Digital assay ingestion (LIMS APIs)
  • Certificate of analysis repository

Gestión de inventario

Track lots from mine stockpiles to ports, warehouses, and smelters with full traceability.

  • Lot-level inventory ledger
  • Blend and split tracking
  • Quality-weighted stock views

Logística y exportaciones

Coordinate transport orders, export documentation, and handovers between operators.

  • Transport order management
  • Export documentation packs
  • Real-time shipment status

Ventas, contratos y finanzas

Digitize sales contracts, invoicing, and settlements with automated valuation and transparent terms.

  • Contract lifecycle management
  • Price participation and deductions
  • Automated invoicing and settlement

Trazabilidad del mineral de punta a punta

From mine portal to final buyer, every transformation of each lot is recorded to deliver verifiable ESG and compliance reporting.

Traceability Flow Diagram

  1. 1 Lot created at mine with origin, GPS, and operator identity.
  2. 2 Samples taken and sent to certified labs for analysis.
  3. 3 Assay results and certificates attached to the digital lot.
  4. 4 Lots blended, split, and transported with chain-of-custody events.
  5. 5 Contracts, deliveries, and invoices linked to traceable lots.
  6. 6 ESG, AML, and compliance checks performed on the full history.

Immutable History

Every event—assay, movement, contract, and payment—is recorded with timestamps and responsible parties.

ESG & AML Controls

Configurable rules monitor red flags such as sanctioned entities, conflict zones, and abnormal pricing patterns.

Buyer Confidence

Industrial buyers can validate lot provenance and compliance status before committing to a purchase.

Sobre la empresa

Somos la empresa que construye el software. Nuestra misión es digitalizar la cadena de suministro de minerales ofreciendo una plataforma segura y escalable para que traders, mineros, fundiciones y laboratorios operen con transparencia, trazabilidad y cumplimiento ESG.

Vision

A world where polymetallic minerals flow through a fully digitized, traceable, and trusted network connecting responsible miners to industrial buyers everywhere.

Who We Serve

Mining companies and small miners, metal refineries and smelters, international traders, certified laboratories, and logistics operators coordinating complex export operations.

What We Enable

Automated valuation, transparent pricing, assured quality through lab integration, and stronger ESG and compliance reporting for all actors in the mineral supply chain.
