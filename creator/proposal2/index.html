Traceability that survives every handover.

Each polymetallic lot carries its own digital passport from mine to final buyer, capturing events, assays, movements and ESG signals.

Traceability workflow

  1. Lot created at mine with origin, ownership and location.
  2. Samples drawn and linked to lab work orders.
  3. Assays and certificates attached to the lot record.
  4. Movements between sites recorded with timestamps and actors.
  5. Contracts, deliveries and invoices bound to lot IDs.
  6. Compliance dashboards consume the complete chain.

Compliance & ESG

Configure rules for AML, sanctions lists, conflict minerals and ESG thresholds, with alerts triggered when events breach policy.

  • Automated screening of counterparties
  • Origin assessments and risk flags
  • Evidence packages for auditors and buyers
