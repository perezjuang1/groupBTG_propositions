End-to-End Mineral Traceability

From mine portal to final buyer, every transformation of each lot is recorded to deliver verifiable ESG and compliance reporting.

Traceability Flow Diagram

  1. 1 Lot created at mine with origin, GPS, and operator identity.
  2. 2 Samples taken and sent to certified labs for analysis.
  3. 3 Assay results and certificates attached to the digital lot.
  4. 4 Lots blended, split, and transported with chain-of-custody events.
  5. 5 Contracts, deliveries, and invoices linked to traceable lots.
  6. 6 ESG, AML, and compliance checks performed on the full history.

Immutable History

Every event—assay, movement, contract, and payment—is recorded with timestamps and responsible parties.

ESG & AML Controls

Configurable rules monitor red flags such as sanctioned entities, conflict zones, and abnormal pricing patterns.

Buyer Confidence

Sobre la empresa

We are a company that commercializes polymetallic minerals. Our mission is to be a trusted partner in global mineral trading, with transparent, efficient and ESG-aligned supply chains.

Vision

A world where polymetallic minerals flow through a fully digitized, traceable, and trusted network connecting responsible miners to industrial buyers everywhere.

Who We Serve

Mining companies and small miners, metal refineries and smelters, international traders, certified laboratories, and logistics operators coordinating complex export operations.

What We Enable

Automated valuation, transparent pricing, assured quality through lab integration, and stronger ESG and compliance reporting for all actors in the mineral supply chain.
