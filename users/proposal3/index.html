Traceability from mine to market.

Every lot carries a digital passport capturing origin, assays, movements, contracts and ESG checks, enabling verifiable, responsible sourcing.

Traceability workflow

  1. Lot creation at mine with GPS, ownership and mine-level ESG data.
  2. Sampling and lab work orders linked to the lot.
  3. Assay results, certificates and variances recorded.
  4. Movements across sites tracked with timestamps and actors.
  5. Contracts, deliveries and invoices bound to lot IDs.
  6. Compliance rules applied across the full history.

Compliance & ESG view

  • AML screening for counterparties and jurisdictions.
  • Sanctioned entity checks and conflict mineral screening.
  • ESG scoring based on origin, emissions and certifications.

Buyers and auditors can access compiled evidence packs showing the entire journey of each lot.
